Glasgow agrees five-year plan to build 6,661 affordable homes — but needs £890m from Scottish Government to deliver it

Glasgow City Council has approved its Strategic Housing Investment Plan for 2026/27 to 2030/31, setting out plans for over 6,000 new affordable homes. The plan has been submitted to the Scottish Government along with a request for £890 million in grant funding. Without that funding, the homes will not be built.

Glasgow's housing emergency: 6,000+ homeless households, 4,200+ in temporary accommodation

Glasgow City Council declared a housing emergency in November 2023. More than 6,000 households currently have live homelessness applications, and over 4,200 are in temporary accommodation each night. The cause is straightforward: demand for housing is far greater than supply.
