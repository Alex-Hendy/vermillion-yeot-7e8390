About
Who we are

YIMBY Glasgow is a grassroots campaign making the case for more homes in Glasgow. We are volunteers. We have no funding. We just think Glasgow needs a lot more housing and that the arguments for building it deserve to be made clearly and publicly.

What we are

YIMBY Glasgow is an independent campaign, not affiliated with any political party, developer, or industry body. We take no money from anyone with a financial interest in housing development. Our positions are based on evidence, not on who is paying us — because nobody is.

YIMBY stands for Yes In My Back Yard. It is a direct response to NIMBYism — the tendency of people who already have homes to oppose the building of new ones. We think that tendency has done enormous damage to Glasgow and to Scotland, and we want to make the counter-argument as loudly and clearly as we can.

What we believe

Glasgow has a housing crisis. The cause is a shortage of homes. The solution is building significantly more of them — across all tenures, but primarily through market-rate housebuilding, which is the only mechanism capable of delivering homes at the scale the city needs. You can read the full argument on the ideas page.
