How donations are used

YIMBY Glasgow's running costs are currently minimal — domain registration and small operational expenses, covered personally by volunteers. Donations go toward expanding the campaign: events, printed materials, outreach, and eventually paid coordination time so the campaign can grow beyond what volunteers alone can sustain.

Every donation will be recorded on our funding page.

Other ways to help

The most valuable thing you can do right now costs nothing: respond to planning applications, write to your councillor, share the myth-busting pages, and talk to people. See our get involved page for specifics.
