Support us
Donate to Glasgow YIMBY

Glasgow YIMBY is a volunteer-run campaign with no external funding. If you believe Glasgow needs more homes and that someone should be making that case loudly and publicly, we'd welcome your support.

Donation mechanism coming soon.

We're working on setting up a way to accept donations. In the meantime, if you'd like to support the campaign, drop us an email and we'll let you know when it's ready.

How donations will be used

Glasgow YIMBY's running costs are currently minimal — domain registration and small operational expenses, covered personally by volunteers. Donations would go toward expanding the campaign: events, printed materials, outreach, and eventually paid coordination time so the campaign can grow beyond what volunteers alone can sustain.

Every donation will be recorded on our funding page. We will never accept money from anyone with a direct financial interest in planning decisions or housing development in Glasgow.

Other ways to help

The most valuable thing you can do right now costs nothing: respond to planning applications, write to your councillor, share the myth-busting pages, and talk to people. See our get involved page for specifics.
