Transparency
Our funding

YIMBY Glasgow has received no external funding or donations. The campaign is run entirely by volunteers, with small running costs covered personally by the people involved.

The full picture

YIMBY Glasgow has never received money from a developer, a political party, an industry body, or any other external source. We have accepted no donations. Nobody is paying us to make these arguments.

The only costs the campaign has incurred are small operational expenses — domain registration, hosting, and email — which have been covered out of pocket by volunteers. These costs amount to a modest annual sum and confer no influence over what we say or who we say it for.

Item Cost Paid by
Domain registration (glasgowyimby.com) £10/year Volunteer (personal)
Website hosting £0 (GitHub Pages) n/a
Google Workspace (email) £7/month Volunteer (personal)
Total annual running costs ~£94/year

External donations

YIMBY Glasgow now accepts donations via our donate page. Every donation above a de minimis threshold will be recorded on this page. We will never accept money from anyone with a direct financial interest in planning decisions or housing development in Glasgow.

If you'd like to support the campaign, you can donate here or find other ways to help on our get involved page.
