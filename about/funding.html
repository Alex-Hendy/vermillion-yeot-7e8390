Transparency
Our funding

Glasgow YIMBY has received no external funding or donations. The campaign is run entirely by volunteers, with small running costs covered personally by the people involved.

The full picture

Glasgow YIMBY has never received money from a developer, a political party, an industry body, or any other external source. We have accepted no donations. Nobody is paying us to make these arguments.

The only costs the campaign has incurred are small operational expenses — domain registration and hosting — which have been covered out of pocket by volunteers. These costs amount to a modest annual sum and confer no influence over what we say or who we say it for.

Item Cost Paid by
Domain registration (glasgowyimby.com) ~£15/year Volunteer (personal)
Website hosting £0 (GitHub Pages) n/a

External donations

We are working on setting up a mechanism to accept donations. If and when we do, every donation above a de minimis threshold will be recorded on this page. We will never accept money from anyone with a direct financial interest in planning decisions or housing development in Glasgow.

If you'd like to support the campaign in the meantime, the best thing you can do is get involved.
