Demand 4 — Glasgow City Council
Abolish parking minimums

Glasgow's Supplementary Guidance SG 11 requires a minimum of one car parking space per dwelling. These standards are set arbitrarily, add cost, reduce density, lock in car dependency, and are subject to discretionary negotiation.
